Getting Started

Follow these simple steps to start your mindful productivity journey with ZenTimer.

  1. Download ZenTimer
    Get ZenTimer from the App Store on your iOS device. No account creation required!
  2. Open the App
    Launch ZenTimer and you'll see the beautiful circular timer interface ready to use.
  3. Set Your Timer
    Drag the circular handle to set your focus session from 1 to 60 minutes.
  4. Choose Notification Type
    Select from vibration, flash, sound, or silent alerts in the settings.
  5. Enable Focus Mode (Optional)
    Connect with iOS Focus modes for completely distraction-free sessions.
  6. Start Focusing
    Tap to start your timer and enjoy your mindful productivity session!
