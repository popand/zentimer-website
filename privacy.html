Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority

Last Updated: January 18, 2025

Privacy-First Promise: ZenTimer is designed with complete privacy in mind. We collect zero personal data, work entirely offline, and require no account creation.

1. Introduction

Welcome to ZenTimer, the mindful productivity timer for iOS. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

ZenTimer is built on the principle of complete user privacy. We believe that your focus sessions, productivity habits, and usage patterns should remain private to you.

2. Information We DON'T Collect

ZenTimer is designed to collect zero personal data. We do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal identification information
  • Usage statistics or analytics
  • Timer session data
  • Focus session history
  • Device identifiers
  • Location data
  • Contacts or personal information
  • Any form of user behavior tracking

3. How ZenTimer Works

Completely Offline: ZenTimer operates entirely on your device without any internet connection requirements. All timer functionality works locally.

No Account Required: You can download and use ZenTimer immediately without creating any accounts or providing personal information.

Local Storage Only: Any app preferences or settings are stored locally on your device using iOS's standard app storage mechanisms.

4. iOS Integration & Permissions

ZenTimer integrates with iOS features to enhance your focus experience:

  • Notifications: Used only to alert you when timer sessions complete. No data is transmitted.
  • Focus Modes: Integrates with iOS Focus modes for distraction-free sessions. This integration is handled entirely by iOS.
  • Haptic Feedback: Provides vibration alerts when sessions complete. No data is collected.

All iOS integrations respect Apple's privacy standards and operate without external data transmission.

5. Data Security

Since ZenTimer doesn't collect data, there's no personal information to secure or breach. However, we maintain security best practices:

  • App settings are stored using iOS's secure app storage
  • No network connections or data transmission
  • Regular security reviews of app code
  • Compliance with Apple's App Store security requirements

6. Third-Party Services

No Third-Party Integration: ZenTimer does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

App Store: The only external service interaction is through Apple's App Store for app downloads and updates, which follows Apple's privacy policies.

7. Children's Privacy

ZenTimer is safe for users of all ages. Since we don't collect any personal information, there are no special privacy concerns for children under 13.

Parents can confidently allow their children to use ZenTimer knowing that no personal data is collected or transmitted.

8. International Users

ZenTimer's privacy-first design automatically complies with international privacy regulations including GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy laws because:

  • No personal data is collected
  • No data processing occurs outside your device
  • No data is stored on external servers
  • No cross-border data transfers happen

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Since ZenTimer doesn't collect personal data, most changes will be clarifications or additions to our privacy commitments.

Any updates will be posted on this page with a new "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

10. Your Rights

Since ZenTimer doesn't collect personal data, traditional data rights (access, deletion, portability) don't apply. However, you maintain complete control:

  • Complete Privacy: Your timer sessions are never recorded or transmitted
  • Full Control: All app settings are stored locally and can be reset by deleting the app
  • No Tracking: We cannot and do not track your usage patterns
